About Me

I’m a product manager and creative technologist living in Southern Maine. Since 2016 I have specialized in platform as a service (PaaS) and software as a service (SaaS) products in the education industry.

I’ve previously held roles in learning design, front-end development, print design, and eBook development. I’m also an experienced line cook, amateur woodworker, novice gardener, and terrible surfer.

How I Work

RESEARCH

Define the problem. Understand the user and their journey. Analyze UI and IA. Evaluate the relevant competition. Define success and requirements.

DESIGN

Solve the problem by combining visual design, user interface, and engaging interactions to provide an enjoyable user experience.

BUILD

Translate the design to code to build a responsive, accessible, and scalable product.

REFINE

Test prototypes with actual users to evaluate the product's ability to solve the problem. Make necessary iterations based on user insights and data gathered.
